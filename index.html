This series gives a lot of big messages.

My favourite characters in (La casa de papel)

Berlin



Berlin, Pedro Alonso's character in La Casa de Papel/Money Heist,
is not a hero—far from it.
A heartless bon vivant, Berlin is able to sing "Bella Ciao"
as passionately as he commits acts of violence.Part madman, part leading man,
Berlin is captivating precisely because he's so unpredictable.

Tokyo



Silene Oliveira, better known by her code-name Tokyo, is one of the main
protagonists of the Netflix series Money Heist, as well as narrator of the story.
She is a member of the heist crew set to rob the Royal Mint of Spain.
